Crafted with passion,
rooted in tradition.

Founded in 2012 by Chef Jimmy John, Saffron & Sage was born from a belief that India's culinary heritage deserves a world stage — without losing its soul.

01

The Beginning

It started with a 12-seat supper club in Mumbai. Word spread faster than we expected. By month three, reservations were booked six weeks out. We knew we had something worth building.

02

Our Philosophy

We cook with restraint and deep respect. Every spice on the plate earns its place. We believe in letting exceptional Indian ingredients speak for themselves, elevated only where it counts.

03

The Producers

We work with 18 local farms from Kashmir to Kerala and 6 international spice suppliers. Every ingredient is traceable. The relationships we've built are as important as the food they produce.

04

Recognition

Three Michelin stars. Asia's 50 Best Restaurant #4. James Beard nominee 2022. But we measure success differently — by the guests who return, and the team that stays.

Meet the Chef

Jimmy John

Executive Chef & Founder · 3 Michelin Stars

Born in Chennai and raised on his grandmother's cooking, Jimmy John spent his formative years absorbing the depth and complexity of South Indian cuisine before travelling to Europe to train under some of the world's greatest chefs.

At elBulli under Ferran Adrià, he discovered the art of deconstruction. At Alain Ducasse's Louis XV, he learned the power of restraint. He returned to India in 2010 with a clear mission: to build a restaurant that could sit alongside the world's best — while staying unmistakably Indian.

Today, his 12-course tasting menu at Saffron & Sage is considered one of the most ambitious and moving dining experiences in Asia.
